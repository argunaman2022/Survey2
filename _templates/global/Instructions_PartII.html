You will see the same tasks again. Whereas in Part I you guessed the performance of past study participants, in Part II, we are asking you to guess the answers of those who have already completed Part I. Therefore, here the correct answer is the true average guess of others in Part I.

Your Payment
Payment rules are the same as before.

In this randomly chosen task, the closer your guess is to the true value, the higher your bonus payment will be.
